North Carolina Trees


Yellow Poplar

Liriodendron tulipifera

The Yellow Poplar is the tallest hardwood in the eastern US. Its bark starts smooth but becomes rough and brownish-gray as it matures.


Though it grows quickly and is prone to limb breakage in winter, it thrives in sunny, well-drained soil and needs plenty of space.
