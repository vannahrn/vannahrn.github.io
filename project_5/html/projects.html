Girl Time

COMM638, Spring 2024


Using Unity game engine, I created an experience to encapsulate the everyday stressors of living with a chronic illness. More specifically, I wanted to capture how my life feels living with Poly Cystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS). This race-against-time complete-all-the-tasks girly game is my proudest work!


Users have to complete a certain number of daily tasks (i.e., eat, take medicine, brush teeth, etc) in a given time frame. Completing tasks will make the completition bar go up. Completing nonoptimal tasks will cause the bar to shoot down significantly. Even when users do complete the optimal tasks, the completion bar slowly depletes. At the end of the timer, the user will be judged on their completion. There are three outcomes: bad, okay, good.
